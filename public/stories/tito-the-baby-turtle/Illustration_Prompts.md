# ReadAloud Illustration Prompt Guide
## Story: Tito the Baby Turtle — All 12 Pages
### Optimized for Gemini Image Generation

---

## How to Use This Guide

**Step 1:** Copy the Master Style Prompt and paste it into Gemini first as context.

**Step 2:** For each page, copy the Page Prompt and paste it into Gemini. Generate, review, and regenerate if needed.

**Step 3:** Save each image as `page-01.webp`, `page-02.webp`, etc.

**Step 4:** Also save page 1 as `cover.jpg` for the library view.

---

## Tips for Consistent Characters

This book has one main character (Tito) and a cameo friend (the little fish). The CARIBBEAN BEACH is the constant setting.

- **Tito's look:** A tiny baby sea turtle — round, sweet face with big dark eyes, a small smooth shell (green/olive), four little flippers. He should look gentle, brave, and adorable. He has a slightly worried/shy expression in early pages that grows into pure joy by the end.
- **The beach setting:** A gorgeous Caribbean beach — pale golden sand, crystal-clear turquoise water, gentle waves. Palm trees may appear in the background. The sky is brilliant blue with occasional white clouds. Everything is bright, warm, and tropical.
- **The little fish (page 6):** A small, colorful tropical fish — bright orange and white stripes, or blue and yellow — with a friendly smile. Just a brief cameo but charming.
- **Visual arc:** Pages 1–4 are on the beach (dry). Pages 5–9 are entering and swimming in the water (progressively deeper). Pages 10–11 are the wave adventure. Page 12 is happy in the ocean.
- **Key emotional arc:** Tito goes from timid and uncertain → curious and brave → triumphant and joyful. His expressions should clearly show this progression.

Clean outlines with brilliant Caribbean colors — turquoise and deep blue ocean, brilliant white and gold sand, vivid tropical sky, lush greens. Tito the baby sea turtle has big expressive eyes, a small round shell, and tiny cute flippers. The beach setting is a gorgeous Dominican/Caribbean coast. 16:9 landscape format. Style feels bright, clear, and full of tropical wonder. Every image should feel like a perfect sunny beach day, full of adventure and discovery for a tiny brave turtle.

### PAGE 1 — Save as: `page-01.webp`
**Story text:** "Tito is a little baby turtle on the beach."

**Prompt:**
```
Children's book illustration, 16:9 landscape. A gorgeous Caribbean beach scene — pale gold sand, brilliant turquoise water, bright blue sky, a few palm trees in the background. In the center of the sandy beach sits Tito — a tiny, adorable baby sea turtle with big dark expressive eyes, a small round green-olive shell, and four little flippers. He sits on the warm sand looking around curiously and a little unsure. He is very small — emphasize his smallness against the wide beach. The scene should feel warm, bright, and beautiful. Master style: vibrant children's book illustration, soft digital painting, 16:9.
```

> **Tip:** This is the ESTABLISHING PAGE — introduce Tito and the gorgeous beach setting. The contrast between tiny Tito and the wide beautiful beach sets the scale of his big adventure.

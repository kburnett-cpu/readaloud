# Illustration Prompt Guide: Jesus Walks on Water

## Master Style

MASTER STYLE: Vibrant, warm children's book illustration in soft digital painting style. Clean outlines with rich saturated colors. Set in an ancient Middle Eastern Biblical landscape with sandy shores, calm blue water, stone paths, and golden-hour light. Jesus is tall with warm olive skin, long dark brown hair, a short beard, and wears flowing white robes with a light tan sash. His disciples are a group of six to eight men of various ages with olive and brown skin tones, wearing simple earth-toned tunics and robes in colors of rust, blue, and green. The wooden fishing boat is sturdy and weathered, with a single mast and rough-hewn oars. All characters have expressive, friendly faces designed for young children. 16:9 landscape format.

## Page Prompts

### Page 1 — `page-01.webp`
> Jesus told his helpers to get in a boat.

Page 1: Jesus stands on a sunny sandy shoreline near a calm blue lake, smiling warmly and gesturing toward a weathered wooden fishing boat at the water's edge. He is tall with warm olive skin, long dark brown hair, and flowing white robes, and his expression is kind and confident. A group of six disciples in colorful earth-toned tunics gather around him, listening attentively with curious and eager faces. The scene is bathed in warm golden afternoon light, with sandy hills and a clear blue sky in the background. The mood is cheerful, peaceful, and purposeful. Children's book illustration, 16:9 landscape.

### Page 2 — `page-02.webp`
> He said, Go across the big lake. I will come later.

Page 2: Jesus stands on the shore with one hand raised gently, pointing out across the wide shimmering blue lake toward the distant horizon. His white robes catch the warm sunlight and his face is calm and reassuring with a gentle smile. The disciples stand close together near the boat, nodding and looking across the water, some shielding their eyes to see the far shore. Soft golden light stretches across the calm lake surface, and fluffy white clouds drift in a bright blue sky. The mood is warm, trusting, and full of anticipation. Children's book illustration, 16:9 landscape.

### Page 3 — `page-03.webp`
> The helpers got in the boat and rowed and rowed.

Page 3: A sturdy weathered wooden fishing boat moves across a wide blue lake under a fading golden sky. Six disciples in rust, blue, and green tunics lean into large wooden oars, rowing with focused effort, some with determined expressions and others smiling as they work together. The water sparkles with warm light and gentle ripples spread out from the oars. The distant shoreline is barely visible behind them, and the mood is cheerful, industrious, and adventurous. Children's book illustration, 16:9 landscape.

### Page 4 — `page-04.webp`
> Then a big storm came. The wind blew hard.

Page 4: Dark dramatic storm clouds roll in rapidly across a sky that shifts from gray-purple to deep blue, and fierce wind bends the air visibly with swirling lines. The wooden boat is now in the middle of the lake, and the disciples grip the sides and oars with wide eyes and worried expressions. Waves begin to rise and chop around the boat, and loose fabric from their tunics whips in the wind. Lightning flickers in the distant storm clouds, casting an eerie pale glow over the water. The mood is suddenly tense, dramatic, and scary for young readers. Children's book illustration, 16:9 landscape.

### Page 5 — `page-05.webp`
> The water got rough. The boat rocked back and forth.

Page 5: The wooden boat tilts dramatically to one side as large dark waves crash around it and white foam sprays across the deck. The disciples cling to the mast, the sides of the boat, and each other, their faces showing wide-eyed fear and surprise as water splashes over the bow. Their colorful tunics are wet and windblown, and oars are tossed about on the rocking deck. The surrounding lake is a churning mass of deep blue and green waves under a stormy dark sky. The mood is chaotic, frightening, and urgent. Children's book illustration, 16:9 landscape.

### Page 6 — `page-06.webp`
> It was dark. They were scared. They did not know what to do.

Page 6: The scene is very dark, lit only by faint moonlight breaking through stormy clouds and distant flickers of lightning over the churning lake. Inside the tossing boat, the disciples huddle together in a tight group, holding each other with trembling hands and fearful wide eyes, some covering their mouths in fright. Their faces are pale and shadowed in the dim light, and the stormy water crashes all around the rocking vessel. There is no shore visible in any direction, only dark water and threatening clouds. The mood is deeply frightened, helpless, and tense. Children's book illustration, 16:9 landscape.

### Page 7 — `page-07.webp`
> Then they saw someone coming. Someone was walking on the water!

Page 7: One disciple at the front of the rocking boat points urgently toward the dark water with a shocked and astonished expression on his face. In the distance across the dark stormy lake, a glowing white figure is visible, walking calmly on top of the churning waves, surrounded by a soft warm radiant light. The other disciples scramble to look, their faces a mix of terror and wide-eyed wonder, some grabbing each other's arms. The stormy sky still swirls above but the glowing figure on the water draws all attention as the central focal point. The mood is astonishing, mysterious, and breathtaking. Children's book illustration, 16:9 landscape.

### Page 8 — `page-08.webp`
> It was Jesus! Jesus was walking on the water!

Page 8: Jesus walks confidently and serenely across the surface of the dark churning water, his white robes glowing with a warm golden-white light that illuminates the waves around his feet. His face is calm, kind, and smiling gently as he approaches the rocking wooden boat where the disciples peer over the sides with giant amazed eyes and open mouths. Each step he takes on the water sends out soft ripples of glowing light. The stormy sky begins to show hints of clearing near him, and the mood is miraculous, joyful, and awe-inspiring. Children's book illustration, 16:9 landscape.

### Page 9 — `page-09.webp`
> Do not be afraid, Jesus said. It is me.

Page 9: Jesus stands beside the rocking boat on the surface of the water, reaching one hand out warmly toward the frightened disciples with a calm, loving smile on his face. His white robes glow softly and his eyes are gentle and reassuring as he looks directly at the wide-eyed disciples leaning over the boat's edge. The disciples' expressions begin to shift from fear to amazed relief, some reaching their hands back toward him. Soft warm light radiates from Jesus outward across the dark water, and the mood is deeply comforting, tender, and safe. Children's book illustration, 16:9 landscape.

### Page 10 — `page-10.webp`
> The helpers felt safe. The storm stopped.

Page 10: The stormy sky above the lake suddenly breaks open to reveal patches of soft starlight and a glowing moon as the dark clouds roll away and the waves settle into gentle ripples. Jesus stands in the boat surrounded by all the disciples, his white robes glowing warmly in the clearing light, and his arms are open wide with a joyful and loving expression. The disciples smile with huge relief, some laughing, some hugging each other, some reaching toward Jesus with grateful hands. The lake is calm and silver-blue under the clearing sky, and the mood is joyful, peaceful, and deeply relieved. Children's book illustration, 16:9 landscape.

### Page 11 — `page-11.webp`
> Jesus is with us. We do not have to be afraid.

Page 11: The wooden boat glides peacefully across a calm shimmering lake under a beautiful golden sunrise sky streaked with pink and orange. Jesus sits at the center of the boat in glowing white robes, surrounded by all six smiling disciples who lean in close to him with happy, content expressions. The water around the boat is smooth and sparkling with morning light, and distant sandy shores and warm hills are visible ahead. The scene radiates safety, togetherness, and joy, with every face beaming with peace and trust. The mood is warm, uplifting, and deeply reassuring for young readers. Children's book illustration, 16:9 landscape.

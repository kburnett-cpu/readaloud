# ReadAloud Illustration Prompt Guide
## Story: Jesus Heals the Man Who Could Not Walk — All 13 Pages
### Optimized for Gemini Image Generation

---

## How to Use This Guide

**Step 1:** Copy the Master Style Prompt and paste it into Gemini first as context.

**Step 2:** For each page, copy the Page Prompt and paste it into Gemini. Generate, review, and regenerate if needed.

**Step 3:** Save each image as `page-01.webp`, `page-02.webp`, etc.

**Step 4:** Also save page 1 as `cover.jpg` for the library view.

---

## Tips for Consistent Characters

This book has 4 key characters and a crowd.

- **Jesus:** Tall, kind man, 30s, warm olive-brown skin, shoulder-length dark brown hair, short dark beard. Wearing simple white or cream robes with a light blue or tan outer cloak. Always radiates warmth, authority, and deep kindness.
- **The paralyzed man:** A young-ish adult man, Middle Eastern features, warm olive skin, wearing a simple robe. He lies on a simple woven mat (tan/brown). His face expresses helplessness initially, then dawning wonder, then pure JOY and amazement when healed.
- **The four friends:** Four young men in simple robes, varying ages/looks. All show determination, love, creativity in getting their friend to Jesus. They should always look like a tight group.
- **The crowd:** Dense crowd of people in ancient Middle Eastern dress packed into and around a stone house.
- **The house:** An ancient stone house — flat roof (important for the rooftop scenes), thick stone walls, arched doorway packed with people.

## MASTER STYLE PROMPT

Copy and paste this into Gemini BEFORE your first image:

```
MASTER STYLE: Warm, vibrant children's book illustration in soft digital painting style. Clean outlines with rich, warm colors — warm stone beige, Mediterranean sky blue, earth tones, touches of bright color in clothing. Ancient Middle Eastern setting — stone houses, dusty streets, flat rooftops. Characters wear simple ancient robes in creams, blues, and earth tones. Jesus is kind and tall in white robes. 16:9 landscape format. Style is warm, reverent, and joyful — appropriate for preschool children. The miracle moment should feel luminous and filled with wonder. Every image should feel warm, human, and full of faith and love.
```

### PAGE 1 — Save as: `page-01.webp`
**Story text:** "One day, Jesus was teaching inside a house that was full of people."

**Prompt:**
```
Children's book illustration, 16:9 landscape. Interior of an ancient stone house, absolutely packed with people. Jesus (tall, white robes, dark hair, kind face, warm olive skin) stands in the center, speaking and teaching. People are seated on the floor, standing along the walls, leaning in from every direction — the house is completely full. Faces are attentive, awed, eager. Light comes from oil lamps and windows. The house has stone walls, simple wooden furniture. Outside, people who couldn't get in are visible through the doorway. The energy is warm and intent. Master style: vibrant children's book illustration, soft digital painting, 16:9.
```

> **Tip:** Show the house completely PACKED — people everywhere. This sets up why the four friends can't get in through the door.

### PAGE 6 — Save as: `page-06.webp`
**Story text:** "They dug a hole in the roof and slowly lowered their friend down inside."

**Prompt:**
```
Children's book illustration, 16:9 landscape. Split view or angled view: on the flat roof, the four friends dig and pull back the roof material (ancient roofs were made of packed mud/thatch over beams) to create a hole. Below, looking up, we can see through the hole into the crowded house — Jesus and the crowd looking up in amazement as the mat with the paralyzed man slowly descends through the hole on ropes held by the four friends on the roof. The paralyzed man descends with a mixture of nervous anticipation and hope. The crowd below parts and stares upward. Master style: vibrant children's book illustration, soft digital painting, 16:9.
```

> **Tip:** This is the most DRAMATIC image in the book — the man being lowered through the roof. Show both the rooftop effort AND the view from inside looking up, if possible. The crowd's amazement below is priceless.

## AFTER YOU'RE DONE

- Check character consistency: Does Jesus look the same throughout? Do the four friends look like the same group? Does the healed man look consistent?
- Check the mat: The same simple woven mat should be carried, lowered, and then picked up by the healed man.
- Check the house: Same stone house inside and outside throughout.
- Check the emotional arc: Man goes from helpless on mat → receiving miracle → standing → walking → joyful reunion.

# ReadAloud Illustration Prompt Guide
## Story: The Big Storm — All 14 Pages
### Optimized for Gemini Image Generation

---

## How to Use This Guide

**Step 1:** Copy the Master Style Prompt and paste it into Gemini first as context.

**Step 2:** For each page, copy the Page Prompt and paste it into Gemini. Generate, review, and regenerate if needed.

**Step 3:** Save each image as `page-01.webp`, `page-02.webp`, etc.

**Step 4:** Also save page 1 as `cover.jpg` for the library view.

---

## Tips for Consistent Characters

This book has three main characters: the narrator child, Mama, and Papa. There's also a little dog.

- **Child (narrator):** Dominican child, about 4–5, warm brown skin, dark curly hair, bright eyes. Wearing everyday play clothes. Expressive — goes from carefree → scared → comforted → delighted.
- **Mama:** Dominican woman, 30s, warm brown skin, dark hair (pulled back), capable and comforting. Wearing a simple dress or blouse. Mama is the comforter — always reassuring and warm.
- **Papa:** Dominican man, 30s, warm brown skin, short dark hair. Calm, knowledgeable, reassuring. He's the one who watches the sky wisely and explains things.
- **The little dog:** A scruffy, lovable mixed-breed Dominican dog — tan or brown, floppy ears, a bit of a coward (hides from thunder), very cute. Should appear on relevant pages.
- **The house:** A colorful Dominican home with a roof you can hear rain on, windows with shutters, a cozy living room/couch inside.
- **VISUAL ARC (most important):** This book has a clear lighting arc — pages 1–2 are bright daylight → pages 3–10 are darkening (storm inside) → page 11 is quiet/dim → pages 12–14 burst into brilliant post-storm sunlight and rainbow.

## MASTER STYLE PROMPT

Copy and paste this into Gemini BEFORE your first image:

```
MASTER STYLE: Vibrant, warm children's book illustration in soft digital painting style. Clean outlines with rich Caribbean colors. Dominican family characters with warm brown skin, dark curly hair, expressive faces. Setting is a colorful Dominican home and Caribbean landscape with tall green mountains. 16:9 landscape format. The story has a dramatic storm arc — early pages are bright Caribbean daylight, storm pages are dramatically lit with dark blues and grays (but still warm inside the home), and the final pages burst into brilliant sunshine and rainbow. Every image should feel emotionally clear and child-friendly, even the dramatic storm pages.
```

### PAGE 1 — Save as: `page-01.webp`
**Story text:** "One afternoon, big dark clouds roll in over the tall green mountains."

**Prompt:**
```
Children's book illustration, 16:9 landscape. A wide view of a lush Dominican Caribbean landscape — tall, green mountains in the background, a colorful neighborhood in the foreground. The sky is dramatic: on one side, dark gray-purple storm clouds are rolling in over the mountains, massive and ominous but beautiful. The other part of the sky is still bright blue. The contrast between the dark storm clouds and the lush green mountains is striking. The scene is outdoors, warm afternoon light still present but beginning to dim. A Dominican child (4–5, warm brown skin, dark curly hair) may be visible playing outside, looking up at the sky. Master style: vibrant children's book illustration, soft digital painting, 16:9.
```

> **Tip:** This is the OPENING IMAGE — the drama of the storm arriving. The dark clouds rolling over the green mountains should be visually spectacular. Establish the setting clearly.

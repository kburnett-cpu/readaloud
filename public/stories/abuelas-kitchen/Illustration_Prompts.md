# ReadAloud Illustration Prompt Guide
## Story: Grandma's Kitchen (Abuela's Kitchen) — All 13 Pages
### Optimized for Gemini Image Generation

---

## How to Use This Guide

**Step 1:** Copy the Master Style Prompt and paste it into Gemini first as context.

**Step 2:** For each page, copy the Page Prompt and paste it into Gemini. Generate, review, and regenerate if needed.

**Step 3:** Save each image as `page-01.webp`, `page-02.webp`, etc.

**Step 4:** Also save page 1 as `cover.jpg` for the library view.

---

## Tips for Consistent Characters

This book has two main characters — a young child (the narrator) and their grandmother (Abuela). The WARMTH AND COZINESS of the kitchen is the #1 visual constant.

- **Child's look:** Dominican child, about 4–5 years old, warm brown skin, dark curly hair, wide curious eyes, big smile. Wearing a small floral apron tied over everyday clothes.
- **Abuela's look:** Dominican grandmother, 60s–70s, warm brown skin, white or silver hair pulled back in a bun, round and soft-faced with deep laugh lines, wearing a well-loved colorful apron (floral or striped) over a simple housedress. Big, loving presence.
- **Kitchen setting:** A warm Dominican kitchen — terracotta tile floor, colorful painted cabinets, a heavy pot on a gas stove, wooden cutting board, a window with curtains letting in morning light. Onions, garlic, and peppers on the counter.
- **Visual anchor:** Abuela's floral apron and the big dark pot on the stove should appear in almost every kitchen scene.
- **Emotional arc:** Pages 1–2 are arrival. Pages 3–13 are warm cooking together. The mood should build from curious and excited → joyful and proud → deeply warm family love at the end.

## MASTER STYLE PROMPT

Copy and paste this into Gemini BEFORE your first image:

```
MASTER STYLE: Vibrant, warm children's book illustration in soft digital painting style. Clean outlines with rich, saturated Caribbean colors — warm golds, tropical terracotta reds, kitchen greens, cheerful yellows. Characters are Dominican with warm brown skin, dark curly or wavy hair, expressive faces full of joy. Setting is a colorful, cozy Dominican kitchen and neighborhood. 16:9 landscape format. Style feels like a modern animated film — bright, clear, and full of warmth and detail. Every image should feel like a warm hug, inviting a 4-year-old child into a grandmother's loving kitchen.
```

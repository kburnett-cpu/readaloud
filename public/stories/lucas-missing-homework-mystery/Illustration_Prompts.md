# Illustration Prompt Guide: Luca's Missing Homework Mystery

## Master Style

MASTER STYLE: Vibrant, warm children's book illustration in soft digital painting style. Clean outlines with rich saturated colors. Main character Luca is a cheerful 8-year-old Dominican boy with warm brown skin, short curly black hair, wearing a white collared school uniform shirt and navy blue shorts with white sneakers. His friends are: Noah, a tall 8-year-old Dominican boy with deep brown skin, neat short twists, wearing a white school uniform shirt and navy pants; Sophia, a bright-eyed 8-year-old Dominican girl with medium brown skin, long black hair in two puff ponytails, wearing a white blouse and navy blue skirt; Holly, a petite 8-year-old Dominican girl with warm brown skin, straight black hair in a braid, wearing a white blouse and navy skirt with small flower clips; Yeibel, an energetic 8-year-old Dominican girl with rich dark brown skin, curly hair pulled into a high puff, wearing a white blouse and navy skirt with colorful friendship bracelets. Señora Martínez is a kind Dominican woman in her 40s with warm brown skin, dark hair pulled into a bun, wearing a bright floral blouse. Setting is Santo Domingo, Dominican Republic — colorful painted homes in yellow, coral, turquoise, and green with iron balconies, lush tropical plants, mango trees, bougainvillea, and a warm golden Caribbean light. Hope Academy school features cheerful painted classrooms with large windows, tropical courtyard, wooden benches, and vibrant student artwork on the walls. 16:9 landscape format.

# Illustration Prompt Guide: A Butterfly Begins

## Master Style

MASTER STYLE: Vibrant, warm children's book illustration in soft digital painting style. Clean outlines with rich saturated colors. Main character is Lucia, a cheerful Dominican girl about 6 years old, with warm brown skin, big dark brown eyes, and thick black hair in two pom-pom puffs tied with yellow ribbons. She wears a bright coral-pink short-sleeve shirt and light denim shorts with white sneakers. Setting is a lush Caribbean yard in the Dominican Republic with tropical plants, broad banana leaves, colorful bougainvillea in magenta and orange, a bright turquoise-painted wooden home with white trim visible in the background, warm golden sunlight, and rich green grass. Family members share warm brown skin tones and dark hair. 16:9 landscape format.

## Page Prompts

### Page 1 — `page-01.webp`
> Lucia ran outside to play in her bright green yard.

Lucia, a cheerful Dominican girl with warm brown skin, thick black pom-puff pigtails tied with yellow ribbons, wearing a coral-pink shirt and denim shorts, runs joyfully across a lush bright green tropical yard. Her arms are spread wide and her face beams with a huge open smile. The background shows a vibrant turquoise Dominican home with white trim, magenta bougainvillea climbing the fence, and tall banana leaf plants swaying gently. Warm golden afternoon sunlight floods the scene, casting soft shadows on the green grass. Children's book illustration, 16:9 landscape.

### Page 2 — `page-02.webp`
> Under a big leafy plant, she found a tiny green caterpillar.

Lucia crouches down low near a large leafy tropical plant with broad shiny green leaves, her eyes wide with wonder and delight. Nestled on a thick green leaf close to the ground is a tiny bright green caterpillar, no bigger than Lucia's finger. Lucia's face is inches from the leaf, her mouth forming a small surprised 'oh' of excitement. Dappled sunlight filters through the canopy of leaves above, casting playful light spots across the scene. Children's book illustration, 16:9 landscape.

### Page 3 — `page-03.webp`
> The little caterpillar munched and munched on the soft, juicy leaves.

A close-up and slightly wide view of the tiny bright green caterpillar with small yellow stripes sitting on a large glossy tropical leaf, munching contentedly with small visible bite marks along the leaf edge. Lucia kneels nearby in the background, chin resting on her folded hands, watching with a delighted grin. The leaf is vibrantly green and oversized, filling much of the frame to emphasize the caterpillar's small world. Warm dappled sunlight highlights the textures of the leaf and the caterpillar's chubby little body. Children's book illustration, 16:9 landscape.

### Page 4 — `page-04.webp`
> Every morning, Lucia ran outside to check on her new little friend.

Lucia runs eagerly across the dewy morning yard toward the big leafy plant, one arm stretched forward and her sneakers kicking up tiny drops of morning dew from the bright green grass. Her pom-puff pigtails bounce behind her and her face shows excited anticipation with a wide smile. The scene is bathed in soft early morning golden light with a pale blue sky visible above the colorful turquoise home in the background. A rooster can be seen in the far background near a garden fence draped in bougainvillea. Children's book illustration, 16:9 landscape.

### Page 5 — `page-05.webp`
> She watched the caterpillar grow bigger and fatter each sunny day.

Lucia and the caterpillar are shown side by side in a warm split-focus composition — Lucia kneeling close with her hands on her knees and a proud happy grin, while the caterpillar on its broad leaf is noticeably plumper and longer than before. Lucia holds her fingers slightly apart to show how much it has grown, her dark eyes shining with pride. The tropical yard glows in bright midday Caribbean sunshine with vivid greens and golden light. Children's book illustration, 16:9 landscape.

### Page 6 — `page-06.webp`
> One day, the caterpillar began to wrap itself in silky white threads.

The plump green caterpillar clings to a thick plant stem and begins weaving itself in delicate silvery-white silk threads that catch the light and shimmer gently. Lucia watches from very close, her face showing fascinated awe — mouth slightly open, eyes wide and sparkling. The silk threads are rendered beautifully, fine and luminous against the deep green background of tropical leaves. Soft warm afternoon light glows through the foliage behind them. Children's book illustration, 16:9 landscape.

### Page 7 — `page-07.webp`
> Soon it was hidden inside a small, smooth, green cocoon on the stem.

A small smooth oval cocoon in muted jade green hangs perfectly still from a thick plant stem, wrapped tightly and neat. Lucia stands back slightly, hands clasped together at her chest, studying the cocoon with a thoughtful and curious expression. The surrounding tropical leaves are lush and quiet, the yard peaceful and still in warm afternoon light. The cocoon is centered in the composition, small but significant, surrounded by soft bokeh greens. Children's book illustration, 16:9 landscape.

### Page 8 — `page-08.webp`
> Lucia waited and waited, but the cocoon stayed very still and quiet.

Lucia sits cross-legged in the grass in front of the plant, chin resting in both palms, staring at the very still and quiet green cocoon with a patient but slightly impatient expression — eyebrows slightly furrowed, lips pursed. The cocoon hangs motionless on its stem. Time feels suspended; the yard is calm and golden in late afternoon light. A small butterfly can be seen in the far background flying near flowers, hinting at what is to come. Children's book illustration, 16:9 landscape.

### Page 9 — `page-09.webp`
> Mamá told her, some good things just need a little more time.

Lucia's warm brown-skinned Mamá, a loving Dominican woman with long dark wavy hair, a floral yellow and orange blouse, and a gentle smile, kneels down beside Lucia in the yard. Mamá places one hand warmly on Lucia's shoulder and gestures softly toward the cocoon with the other hand, speaking with kind reassurance. Lucia looks up at Mamá with a thoughtful expression, beginning to understand. The scene is warm and tender, bathed in soft golden evening light. Children's book illustration, 16:9 landscape.

### Page 10 — `page-10.webp`
> One bright morning, the cocoon began to wiggle and shake and crack open.

Early morning bright light fills the yard as Lucia leans in close to the cocoon, eyes enormous with excitement — the cocoon is visibly wiggling and cracking open at the top, with tiny splits of light showing through the green shell. Lucia's hands are pressed to her cheeks in thrilled disbelief, her pom-puff pigtails quivering as if she herself is trembling with anticipation. The background glows in brilliant Caribbean morning sunshine with vivid turquoise and magenta colors. Children's book illustration, 16:9 landscape.

### Page 11 — `page-11.webp`
> Out came a beautiful orange and yellow butterfly, drying its new wings.

A breathtaking orange and yellow butterfly with bold black-veined wings clings to the broken cocoon shell on the stem, slowly stretching and drying its gorgeous new wings in the warm sunlight. The wings are large, luminous, and detailed with rich amber, gold, and deep orange patterns. Lucia watches from inches away, her expression pure wonder and joy — eyes glistening, a soft awed smile on her face. Golden sunlight halos the butterfly beautifully against a backdrop of tropical green leaves. Children's book illustration, 16:9 landscape.

### Page 12 — `page-12.webp`
> Lucia smiled and watched it fly up into the warm blue sky.

Lucia stands in the open sunny yard, head tilted back, one hand raised in a gentle wave goodbye, her face radiant with a joyful and proud smile — her eyes following the brilliant orange and yellow butterfly as it soars upward into a glowing warm blue Caribbean sky dotted with soft white clouds. The butterfly is visible high above, wings catching the light magnificently. The colorful turquoise home, tropical flowers, and lush green yard fill the background in a warm celebratory scene. Children's book illustration, 16:9 landscape.

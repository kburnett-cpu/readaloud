# ReadAloud Illustration Prompt Guide
## Story: Jesus Feeds the Five Thousand — All 14 Pages
### Optimized for Gemini Image Generation

---

## How to Use This Guide

**Step 1:** Copy the Master Style Prompt and paste it into Gemini first as context.

**Step 2:** For each page, copy the Page Prompt and paste it into Gemini. Generate, review, and regenerate if needed.

**Step 3:** Save each image as `page-01.webp`, `page-02.webp`, etc.

**Step 4:** Also save page 1 as `cover.jpg` for the library view.

---

## Tips for Consistent Characters

This book has three main visual anchors: Jesus, the little boy, and the crowd.

- **Jesus:** A tall, kind man in his 30s, Middle Eastern features, warm olive-brown skin, shoulder-length dark brown hair, short dark beard, kind dark eyes. Wearing simple white or cream robes with a light blue or tan outer cloak. He should radiate warmth, authority, and deep kindness in every image. His expression is always gentle and loving — never harsh or stern.
- **The little boy:** About 7–8 years old, Middle Eastern child, warm olive skin, dark hair, wearing a simple tunic. He has a small woven basket and carries five small round bread loaves and two small fish. His expression is generous, trusting, and happy. He's the hero of the crowd scenes.
- **The disciples:** A group of 6–12 men in simple robes, various ages, Middle Eastern features. They can look concerned or amazed — followers who are often surprised by Jesus.
- **The crowd:** Thousands of people sitting on a green hillside — men, women, children, in ancient Middle Eastern dress. The crowd should look diverse and large.
- **Visual setting:** An ancient Middle Eastern landscape — grassy hillside, blue sky, the Sea of Galilee visible in some background shots. Late afternoon/sunset light for the miracle pages.

## MASTER STYLE PROMPT

Copy and paste this into Gemini BEFORE your first image:

```
MASTER STYLE: Warm, vibrant children's book illustration in soft digital painting style. Clean outlines with rich, warm colors — Mediterranean greens, dusty gold sand, warm browns and creams, beautiful blue sky and water. Characters wear simple ancient Middle Eastern clothing in earth tones, blues, and creams. Jesus is a kind, tall man in white robes. The setting is an ancient Biblical landscape — grassy hillsides, blue sky, Sea of Galilee. 16:9 landscape format. The style is warm, reverent, and joyful — appropriate for preschool children. Miracle pages should feel luminous and magical. Every image should feel warm, loving, and full of wonder.
```

**Story text:** "One day, a huge crowd of people followed Jesus to a grassy hillside."

**Prompt:**
```
Children's book illustration, 16:9 landscape. A vast, gently rolling grassy hillside in ancient Israel. Thousands of people — men, women, children — in ancient Middle Eastern clothing (earth tones, blues, creams) are streaming toward the hillside from all directions, following Jesus. Jesus (tall, warm olive skin, shoulder-length dark hair, short beard, white robes with blue cloak) walks ahead on the hillside, looking back with a welcoming smile. The crowd is enormous and joyful — people of all ages. The sky is beautiful blue, the grass is green, distant hills visible. Master style: vibrant children's book illustration, soft digital painting, 16:9.
```

> **Tip:** Establish the SCALE — thousands of people on a beautiful hillside following Jesus. The scene should feel joyful and full of energy.

## AFTER YOU'RE DONE

- Check character consistency: Does Jesus look the same throughout (white robes, kind face, warm olive skin, dark hair)? Does the little boy look consistent?
- Check the basket: The little boy's original small woven basket should look the same when it appears.
- Check the twelve large baskets: Should be clearly larger and fuller than the original small basket.
- Check the lighting arc: Pages 1–2 bright midday → pages 3–9 warm golden afternoon → pages 10–14 beautiful golden sunset.

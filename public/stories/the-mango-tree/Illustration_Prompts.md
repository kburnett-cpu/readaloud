# ReadAloud Illustration Prompt Guide
## Story: The Mango Tree — All 12 Pages
### Optimized for Gemini Image Generation

---

## How to Use This Guide

**Step 1:** Copy the Master Style Prompt and paste it into Gemini first as context.

**Step 2:** For each page, copy the Page Prompt and paste it into Gemini. Generate, review, and regenerate if needed.

**Step 3:** Save each image as `page-01.webp`, `page-02.webp`, etc.

**Step 4:** Also save page 1 as `cover.jpg` for the library view.

---

## Tips for Consistent Characters

This book has 4 main characters: the narrator child, Papa, Mama, and little sister.

- **Child (narrator):** Dominican child, about 5–6, warm brown skin, dark hair (short or in small puffs), wearing bright shorts and a colorful T-shirt. Energetic and curious.
- **Papa:** Tall Dominican man, 30s, warm brown skin, short dark hair, friendly strong face, wearing casual clothes — T-shirt, jeans or shorts. Gentle and playful with the child.
- **Mama:** Dominican woman, 30s, warm brown skin, dark hair (may be in braids or pulled back), wearing a colorful dress or blouse. Warm and capable.
- **Sister:** Slightly smaller than the narrator child, similar features. Matching energy — playful and joyful.
- **The Mango Tree:** This tree is practically a character — BIG, lush, with spreading branches heavy with ripe yellow-orange-red mangoes. It should look magnificent and slightly magical.
- **Visual anchor:** The mango tree should be recognizable and consistent across all pages that feature it. Mangoes are the visual star — warm yellow, orange, and red.

## MASTER STYLE PROMPT

Copy and paste this into Gemini BEFORE your first image:

```
MASTER STYLE: Vibrant, warm children's book illustration in soft digital painting style. Clean outlines with rich, saturated tropical Caribbean colors — lush greens, warm mango yellows and oranges, deep blue sky, terracotta soil. Dominican family characters with warm brown skin, dark hair, expressive joyful faces. Setting is a lush Dominican yard with a magnificent mango tree and a warm Caribbean home. 16:9 landscape format. Style feels bright, clear, and full of tropical warmth and joy. Every image should feel like a sunny Caribbean afternoon full of laughter and family love.
```

### PAGE 1 — Save as: `page-01.webp`
**Story text:** "There is a big mango tree in our yard."

**Prompt:**
```
Children's book illustration, 16:9 landscape. A magnificent, large mango tree dominates the center of a lush Dominican yard. The tree is full and spreading, with dark green leaves and dozens of ripe mangoes hanging in clusters — yellow, orange, and red. The yard around it is tropical and colorful — grass, flowering shrubs, a colorful Dominican house visible in the background. A Dominican child (5–6, warm brown skin, dark hair, colorful T-shirt and shorts) stands at the edge of the yard looking up at the tree in total wonder and delight — mouth open, eyes wide. The tree towers over the child, making it feel enormous and magical. Warm Caribbean sunlight. Master style: vibrant children's book illustration, soft digital painting, 16:9.
```

> **Tip:** This is the ESTABLISHING IMAGE — the tree must look magnificent. It should feel like the biggest, most wonderful tree a child has ever seen. This is also your reference image for all future pages featuring the tree.

# Illustration Prompt Guide: The Mapmaker's Secret

## Master Style

MASTER STYLE: Vibrant, warm children's book illustration in soft digital painting style. Clean outlines with rich saturated colors. Main character Ashley is a ten-year-old Dominican girl with warm brown skin, thick black hair in two puff buns, wearing a bright yellow t-shirt and denim shorts with red sneakers. Her friends: Marcos is a brown-skinned boy with short curly black hair and a green striped shirt; Lucianna is a brown-skinned girl with long braided hair and a purple top; Axel is a stocky brown-skinned boy with close-cropped hair and an orange hoodie; Clifford is a tall brown-skinned boy with a wide-brimmed straw hat and a blue plaid shirt. Setting is the Dominican Republic — colorful painted homes, lush tropical vegetation, ceiba trees, wild ferns, mountain valleys, warm Caribbean light. 16:9 landscape format.

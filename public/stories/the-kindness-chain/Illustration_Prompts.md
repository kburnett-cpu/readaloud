# Illustration Prompt Guide: The Kindness Chain

## Master Style

MASTER STYLE: Vibrant, warm children's book illustration in soft digital painting style. Clean outlines with rich saturated colors. Main character Elías is a quiet, gentle Dominican boy, approximately 8 years old, with warm medium-brown skin, short dark curly hair, expressive dark brown eyes, and a soft smile. He wears a crisp white short-sleeve school uniform shirt, navy blue shorts, and worn brown sandals with white socks. The setting is a lively Dominican Republic neighborhood with colorful painted homes in turquoise, coral, and yellow, lush tropical plants including banana leaves, bougainvillea, and palm trees, sun-drenched dirt and cobblestone streets, and warm golden Caribbean light. Supporting characters include warm brown-skinned Dominican townspeople of various ages. The overall mood is tender, hopeful, and luminous with soft golden and warm amber tones throughout. 16:9 landscape format.

## Page Prompts

### Page 1 — `page-01.webp`
> Elías was a quiet boy with a big, warm heart.

A warm portrait-style scene of Elías, a gentle 8-year-old Dominican boy with warm medium-brown skin, short dark curly hair, and expressive dark brown eyes, standing in front of his colorful turquoise family home surrounded by blooming bougainvillea and tropical plants. He holds a small brown paper lunch bag to his chest and gazes softly toward the viewer with a quiet, thoughtful smile. Warm golden morning sunlight filters through banana leaves above him, casting dappled light across his white school uniform shirt. The mood is tender and intimate, introducing a boy whose kindness lives just below the surface. Children's book illustration, 16:9 landscape.

### Page 2 — `page-02.webp`
> Every day he walked past an old man sitting outside his school.

Elías in his white school uniform and navy shorts walks along a sun-drenched cobblestone street lined with colorful Dominican homes in coral, yellow, and turquoise, his brown paper lunch bag swinging gently at his side. In the middle distance, an elderly Dominican man sits on the low concrete steps just outside the iron school gate, his posture slouched and weary, a tattered brown hat resting on his lap. Elías glances at the old man with a gentle, noticing expression, his footsteps slowing slightly. Lush tropical greenery and a bright blue Caribbean sky frame the scene with warmth. Children's book illustration, 16:9 landscape.

### Page 3 — `page-03.webp`
> The old man's clothes were worn, and his eyes looked very tired.

A close, empathetic view of the elderly Dominican man sitting hunched on worn concrete steps outside the school gate, his dark brown skin weathered and lined with age, his clothes faded and patched — a fraying beige guayabera shirt and old gray trousers. His tired, deeply kind eyes stare quietly ahead, holding a lifetime of stories and a quiet sadness. In the soft background, Elías stands at a slight distance, looking at the old man with a gentle and compassionate expression, clutching his lunch bag. Warm morning light bathes the scene, and a single yellow flower grows in a crack in the wall beside the man. Children's book illustration, 16:9 landscape.

### Page 4 — `page-04.webp`
> One morning, Elías looked down at his lunch and made a quiet choice.

Elías sits alone at a small wooden school desk near a classroom window overlooking the sunny courtyard, his brown paper lunch bag sitting open on the desk in front of him. He looks down at his lunch — a wrapped container of rice and beans and a ripe golden mango — with a thoughtful, tender expression, one hand resting gently on his chin as he pauses mid-decision. Warm sunlight streams through the window, illuminating his face and the food with a golden glow. The classroom behind him is cheerful with colorful drawings pinned to the walls, but Elías is quietly still, caught in a private, important moment of the heart. Children's book illustration, 16:9 landscape.

### Page 5 — `page-05.webp`
> He set his brown bag softly beside the old man and walked away without a word.

A gentle, quietly moving scene: Elías crouches down on the sun-warmed cobblestone outside the school gate and places his brown paper lunch bag softly beside the sleeping old man, his face calm and sincere with a small private smile. The old man rests with his chin to his chest, unaware. Elías is already beginning to rise and turn away, never looking back, his white uniform bright in the morning sun. Tropical plants and the colorful school wall surround them, and a small yellow butterfly floats nearby as if witnessing the moment. The mood is tender, selfless, and luminous. Children's book illustration, 16:9 landscape.

### Page 6 — `page-06.webp`
> The old man opened the bag and found rice, beans, and a sweet mango.

A warm, close-up scene of the elderly man's weathered brown hands carefully opening the brown paper lunch bag on his lap, revealing a neatly packed container of white rice and red beans and a perfectly ripe, golden-orange mango glowing in the sunlight. His expression shifts from surprise to quiet wonder, eyes wide and lips parting softly. The cobblestone street and colorful painted walls glow warmly behind him, with bougainvillea blooms framing the top of the scene. The light feels almost sacred, as if the simple food is a small miracle. Children's book illustration, 16:9 landscape.

### Page 7 — `page-07.webp`
> Tears filled his eyes because someone had cared for him that day.

A deeply emotional close-up of the elderly Dominican man's face as he holds the open lunch bag to his chest, his dark kind eyes glistening with tears that catch the warm Caribbean sunlight. His expression is one of profound, overwhelmed gratitude — a soft trembling smile breaking across his weathered face. One tear runs gently down his cheek. In the blurred background, the busy school street continues with children and families passing by, none of them aware of this quiet moment of grace. Warm golden light wraps around him like an embrace. Children's book illustration, 16:9 landscape.

### Page 8 — `page-08.webp`
> Feeling full of hope, the old man helped a woman whose heavy bags had fallen in the street.

A lively Dominican street scene bathed in bright afternoon light, where the elderly man — now walking upright with renewed energy, his old hat back on his head and a gentle smile on his face — reaches down to help a middle-aged Dominican woman in a floral dress whose heavy cloth grocery bags have split and tumbled onto the cobblestones, sending plantains, mangoes, and canned goods rolling across the street. His face is warm and eager to help; her expression moves from distress to grateful surprise. Colorful homes and a fruit market stall glow in the background, full of life and tropical color. Children's book illustration, 16:9 landscape.

### Page 9 — `page-09.webp`
> That woman smiled so brightly that she gave her last coin to a little girl selling flowers.

A joyful street scene where the Dominican woman in her bright floral dress, her groceries now gathered safely in her arms, beams a radiant, full-faced smile that seems to light up the entire street. She leans down to press her last shining gold coin into the tiny outstretched hand of a small Dominican girl, approximately 6 years old, with two puffs of curly hair and a yellow sundress, who stands beside a makeshift wooden cart displaying bundles of cheerful yellow tropical flowers. The little girl's eyes go wide with delight and disbelief. The street around them is colorful and warm, buzzing with Caribbean afternoon life. Children's book illustration, 16:9 landscape.

### Page 10 — `page-10.webp`
> The little girl ran home and surprised her sick mamá with a beautiful yellow bloom.

A joyful, motion-filled scene of the small flower-selling girl in her yellow sundress running barefoot up the path to her modest, brightly painted coral-colored home, her two curly puffs bouncing as she clutches a single perfect yellow tropical bloom in both hands. She bursts through the doorway where her mamá — a tired but beautiful Dominican woman in a simple blue housedress — lies resting on a small daybed, looking pale and unwell. The little girl's face is bursting with love and excitement; her mamá's weary eyes are just beginning to widen in tender surprise. Warm indoor light and potted plants frame the doorway. Children's book illustration, 16:9 landscape.

### Page 11 — `page-11.webp`
> Her mamá felt so loved that she cooked a big pot of soup and shared it with all her neighbors.

A warm, bustling evening scene set in a modest but cheerful Dominican kitchen and spilling out into a small yard strung with lights, where the mamá — now upright and glowing with renewed energy — stirs a giant steaming pot of fragrant golden soup on a small stove. Through the open door and windows, neighbors of all ages — old men, young mothers, children — gather with bowls and smiles, chatting and laughing in the amber evening light. The yard is lush with tropical plants, and the smell of the soup seems to visually shimmer in the warm air. The scene overflows with community, generosity, and Caribbean warmth. Children's book illustration, 16:9 landscape.

### Page 12 — `page-12.webp`
> One of those neighbors was Elías's abuela, who hugged him tight that evening and said, 'God is good.'

A tender, glowing evening scene inside the modest Caribbean home of Elías's abuela — a warm, round-faced Dominican grandmother with silver-streaked hair pulled back softly, wearing a floral housedress — as she wraps Elías in a full, tight hug, her eyes closed with deep contentment. Elías's arms wrap around her, his face tucked against her shoulder with a small peaceful smile. A bowl of golden soup sits on the wooden table behind them, still steaming. The room glows with warm lamplight, and through the open window, a deep violet-blue Caribbean evening sky is dotted with stars. The abuela's lips are parted softly as if mid-whisper. Children's book illustration, 16:9 landscape.

### Page 13 — `page-13.webp`
> Elías smiled, never knowing his one small act of kindness had wrapped love all around his town.

A magical, wide panoramic final scene showing the town of colorful Dominican homes glowing softly under a deep indigo-and-gold evening sky, with a gentle golden light weaving like a ribbon through every part of the neighborhood — past the school gate, the cobblestone streets, the flower cart, the soup-sharing yard, the abuela's window. In the foreground, Elías stands on the small step of his turquoise home, looking out at the glowing town with a calm, unaware smile, his hands in his pockets, his white uniform still crisp. He does not know. But the light — warm, golden, quietly magical — tells the whole story around him. Children's book illustration, 16:9 landscape.
